What is Gonnet?

A smart QR-based system that helps people connect instantly — without sharing personal details publicly.

How It Works
  • Create your QR profile
  • Attach QR to your vehicle or belongings
  • Anyone scans → your profile opens instantly
What You Can Add
  • Name, mobile, email
  • Emergency contact details
  • Social links & personal info
  • Vehicle or other details
Privacy Control
  • You control what is visible
  • Hidden fields stay private
  • No data shared without consent
Access & Edit
  • Login using your email
  • Edit via OTP verification
  • Access anytime via your QR link
Important Notes
  • Each mobile number can be used only once
  • Same email can have multiple profiles
  • You can update your profile anytime
Simple. Secure. Instant.

That’s Gonnet.
